The service focuses heavily on these bet types:

  

**Point Spread Bets**

-   The most common type of bet in American sports betting
-   Cody provides picks where the favorite "gives" points and the underdog "takes" points
-   Example: If Miami Heat are -3.5, they need to win by 4+ points for the bet to hit

  

**Moneyline Bets**

-   Straight-up winner predictions without point spreads
-   Higher payouts on underdog selections
-   Often used in parlay combinations for bigger payouts

  

**Over/Under Bets**

-   Total points scored by both teams combined
-   Popular in NBA and NFL picks
-   Example: If the total is 220.5 in an NBA game, you bet whether the final score exceeds that number

  

**Parlay Bets**

-   Multiple bets combined for higher payouts
-   All selections must win for the parlay to hit
-   Cody's specialty - claims to hit 38 parlays in 31 days
-   Account for 70% of sportsbook revenue nationwide

  

**Proposition Bets**

-   Player-specific statistical outcomes
-   Examples: Will LeBron James score over 25.5 points?
-   Popular during playoff seasons and major events

  

**Live Betting**

-   In-game betting opportunities while events are happening
-   Requires quick decision-making and real-time analysis
